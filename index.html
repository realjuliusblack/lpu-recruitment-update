AI Job Sourcing Engine

  • Market Scanning. Monitors LocumSmart and LotusOne/Aya around the clock. Every new locums opening hits LPU's tracker before a human recruiter sees it.
  • Email Job Parsing. Physician groups email job openings to LPU. Claude reads each one, pulls the specialty, location, facility, and rate, and creates a clean record automatically.
  • AI Job Descriptions. Every raw job gets transformed into a branded, physician-first JD. Scored against the LPU style guide. Ready to publish.
  • Live Job Board. A real-time public board backed by Google Sheets. Sorted by date. Deduplicated. Updated every scrape cycle.
  • Rate Intelligence. Pay rates extracted from every source. Email rates are authoritative and never overwritten. All rates flow into the rate engine downstream.
The Result
LPU goes to market with every available locums opening, from every platform and every direct relationship, without anyone manually looking for jobs.
The Hardest Problem

This Engine Was the Most Complex Piece in the System

WAF Bypass

LocumSmart actively blocks scrapers. We built a cloud browser agent on Fly.io to route around it. Per-platform scrape locks prevent session collisions.

Three Data Sources

LocumSmart (browser), LotusOne/Aya (separate portal), and physician emails (unstructured text). All normalized to one schema without race conditions.

Async Architecture

The pipeline blocked callers for 25 minutes. Cloudflare cut connections at 100 seconds. Full rebuild: 202 response in 2.4s, pipeline runs async, polled via execution ID.

Three Cert Cycles

Certified. Revoked after restructuring. Eight bugs fixed. Re-certified. Every failure became a lesson. Every lesson made the system stronger.

Why This Matters
Every hard infrastructure problem in the Recruitment Agent surfaced in the AI Job Sourcing Engine. We solved all of them here. Every other engine inherits the solution.

In Development

Rate Logic Engine

A dedicated rate intelligence layer being added to the AI Job Sourcing Engine. Makes LPU's pricing smarter and fully automated.

Specialty-Based Margins

Each specialty carries its own margin. Emergency Medicine, Hospitalist, and Urgent Care run at 22%. Most others at 20%. Orthopedic Surgery at 18%. Configurable without a code deploy.

Rate Projection

When a bill rate isn't provided, the engine projects one. It checks historical averages first. If fewer than 5 data points exist, it falls back to MGMA/MedHealthBenchmarks by specialty. Every job leaves with a rate.

Admin Rate Dashboard

Ali and Ahmed can review every job's pay rate, bill rate, and margin in one place. Override any rate inline. See averages per facility. Backfill missing rates with one click. Full visibility into LPU's pricing.

In Development
The Rate Logic Engine is the next build inside the AI Job Sourcing Engine. Email and document rates are always authoritative and never overwritten. Projected rates are clearly labeled with confidence levels so nothing is treated as fact when it's an estimate.
