What the Job Sourcing Agent actually does.

The scout. Pulls from vendors, writes descriptions, posts to the board, watches for stale roles. Runs on its own.

Every few hours: the scout checks LocumSmart and LotusOne for new physician roles. Pulls down whatever is new. 82 of today's 95 live jobs came from LocumSmart, 13 from LotusOne. CRNAs and APPs are pulled too, not just physicians.

For every new role: Claude writes a clean job description in LPU's voice. No corporate fluff, no recruiter-speak. Direct and physician-to-physician.

Then it posts: the role lands on locumsunited.com/jobs-beta within minutes. Dedup logic prevents the same role appearing twice when LocumSmart and LotusOne both list it.

Every morning: a stale-job poller checks for roles that have been on the board too long without activity and quietly archives them. A daily health report tells us what came in, what shipped, what failed.
