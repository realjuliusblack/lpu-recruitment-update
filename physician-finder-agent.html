Agent 2 · Physician Finder

The matchmaker. New job, right doctors, day one.

Walks your existing GHL list. Matches every new job to the doctors whose specialty fits. Sends a short, physician-to-physician note about that job.

In Build

Think of it like a matchmaker.

The analogy
A good matchmaker knows her list. When a new opportunity shows up, she already knows who it's for. Makes the intro. Lets them take it from there.

That's this agent. Your contact list is the roster. Every new job is a new opportunity. It finds the right matches and reaches out. One short note per match per job.

What it does.

Four steps, every time a new job posts.

Step 1
Enrich the list.
Daily pass adds specialty and license data from NPPES to every contact. Your list stays current.
Step 2
Match on new jobs.
Sourcing posts a new job. Agent finds every doctor whose specialty matches.
Step 3
Send the note.
Short email in your voice. No template blast. One match, one message.
Step 4
First batch waits.
Ali approves the first send per job. Once cleared, the rest go out automatically.

Three rules.

Reaching out to your list is high stakes.

1
First batch always waits for Ali.
Per job, the first outreach holds for Ali's one-click approval. Subsequent matches auto-fire.
2
One note per doctor per job.
Duplicate job posted? Same doctor never emailed twice. No spam loops.
3
Test mode on until live.
During rollout, outreach redirects to Julius so we tune tone before a real physician sees anything.
